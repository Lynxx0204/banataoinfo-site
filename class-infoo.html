ICT:

The ICT (Information and Communications Technology) strand is part of the Senior High School curriculum in the Philippines under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track. It focuses on equipping students with skills in programming, web development, networking, and digital communication technologies. This strand prepares students for careers in IT-related fields or further studies in computer science and technology.
